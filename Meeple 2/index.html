About Us

Meeple Technologies Private Limited is a company focusing on applications of vacuum technology in healthcare. Our aim is to design and manufacture safe and effective medical devices in India. Our team in headed by IIT alumni who have over 10 years of experience in medtech product design.

With the current range of breast pumps designed and manufactured in-house in India, we are able to better support the mother and baby brands with quality products and aftersaleservices.
